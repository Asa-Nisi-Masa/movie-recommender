Instructions for preparing your ratings

Movies are recommended based on your ratings. To prepare your IMDb ratings do the following:

  • Login to your IMDb account
  • Go to 'Your Ratings'
  • Look for the three vertical dots â‹®
  • Click and choose 'Export'

Alternatively, you can prepare your own ratings file. For that you need two columns:

  • 'Const' or 'IMDbId' with IMDb movie ids
  • 'Your Rating' with a 1-10 rating for each movie

About the recommender

This recommender was trained on publicly available movie datasets. As a result, it has no knowledge of newer movies (2018 and later).
